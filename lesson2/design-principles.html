Visual Hierarchy

Zeven Designs magnum opus article about visual layouts
    Visual Hierarchy is how a site can be designed to influence the order the eyes see
    This order is created by perception and spacing
    Leveraging this can help the UX designer influence a viewer for example, by having a colored backround and manipulating the margins of a photo, I can draw your eyes to diffrent places
